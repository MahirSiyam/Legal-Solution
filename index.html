Business Law

Business law is a set of rules that governs how businesses operate legally. It covers contracts, employment laws, taxes, and company regulations.

Criminal Law

Criminal law is the body of law that defines crimes and prescribes punishments for offenders. It ensures public safety by prosecuting and penalizing unlawful actions.

Child Support

"Every child deserves love, care, and support to grow and thrive. Child support ensures their needs are met, no matter the circumstances."

Education Law

Education law refers to the legal rules and policies governing schools, students, and teachers. It covers issues like student rights, school funding, and education standards.

Divorce Law

Divorce law governs the legal process of ending a marriage, including property division, child custody, and spousal support. It varies by country and state, with different rules on grounds for divorce and legal procedures.

Tax Law

Tax law is the set of rules that governments use to collect money from individuals and businesses.It includes income tax, sales tax, property tax, and other taxes that help fund public services.
